This is TabShare. To sum up our extension in a few words, TabShare allows you to instantly share and access the tabs of your peers with our request system. If you have some specific pages you want to share with your friends, without having to email each link, TabShare is for you. With the click of a button, all tabs open in chrome will be sent through our database to you friendâ€™s computer allowing them to access these pages. This extension would be useful in a working team at hackathons, who don't want to have to use the same google account in order to transfer tabs to one another. Our team developing the extension are Andy Chan, David Gilman, Billy Zhong, Devin Mui, and Kumaran Ratnam.
